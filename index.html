About

The Canyon Creek Championship is an exclusive semi-annual Disc Golf tournament hosted and run for a group of friends out of Austin, Texas every winter and summer. The first Canyon Creek Championship (CCC) was held on July 1st, 2021 and consisted of three 18-hole Disc Golf courses in one day: Wells Branch DGC, Rivery Park, and Old Settlers Park. More recent Championships have split the rounds over different days and/or shortened the number of rounds played. If you would like to learn more about the past Championships and their winners please navigate to the "Past Championships" section for details.
