The current upcoming Canyon Creek Championship is scheduled for the weekend of July 22nd through July 23rd. The plan is to play three 18-hole courses over two days out near Tyler, Texas. The first two rounds will be played at Blackberry Pines Disc Golf Course in Pittsburg, Texas on the 22nd while the third and final round will be played at the difficult Lindsey Park Disc Golf Course (otherwise known as Dogwood) in Tyler on the 23rd. Currently we have six planned competitors for this CCC - tied for the most we have had at any of our previous championships! We are very excited to try out these new-to-the-tournament courses and see who will be crowned Canyon Creek Champion next!
